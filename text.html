FizCoin Foundation

The Fund helps token holders to solve problems in physics and astronomy. Anyone can solve the problem, send it to the blockchain and get rewarded for the correct answer. Therefore, solving problems turns the olympiad into an interesting journey.

The goal of the FizCoin Foundation is to increase the level of knowledge in the field of physics and astronomy among students around the world. Checking the answers to tasks will be honest and decentralized, and voting in the FizCoin DAO will be an incentive to generate interesting and unusual tasks. Solving the tasks themselves will become an unusual hobby for buying souvenirs and NFT for FIZ tokens.

FIZ Token

The FizCoin token allows the community to own the protocol and actively manage it: create physics and astronomy problems, receive rewards for the correct answer to the problem and vote for interesting and unusual tasks. FIZ owners manage the protocol through a management process on the network.

The FIZ token is a solvent coin that can be bought or sold on decentralized exchanges (DEX), for example on Uniswap (see the Buy/Sell section). For the FIZ token, you can buy souvenirs, NFT and much more in the Polygon blockchain. Also, FIZ can be exchanged for fiat (USDT, USDC, DAI) or for an ETH coin of the Ethereum blockchain, and already transferred to the Ethereum blockchain. For example, take the marketplace Rarible, where you can buy NFT for ETH or MATIC (now Polygon).

FizCoin DAO

FizCoin DAO — a decentralized autonomous organization in which votes are held, according to the results of which the most interesting and unusual tasks are published.

Participants of different statuses and different privileges participate in the DAO.

FizCoin protocol

FIZ is an ERC-20 token used in the FizCoin ecosystem to receive a reward for the correct answer to a problem in physics, astrophysics. The FizCoin protocol is an open source ecosystem.

Since FizCoin is a decentralized ecosystem, it serves several purposes:

Governance FizCoin is the ecosystem’s governance token, allowing FizCoin holders to participate in FizCoin DAO.
Unification of Spend FizCoin is the ecosystem’s utility token, giving all its participants a shared and open currency that can be used without centralized intermediaries.
Unique Access For a large number of solved tasks and received tokens, the participant receives privileges: the status of a creator or even a moderator.
Incentivization For the correct solved tasks, the participant receives FIZ tokens, which he can sell.

How does FizCoin work?

The FizCoin smart contract protocol runs on a decentralized blockchain Polygon, which means everyone can join the project and start solving problems.

Solving and sending problems

Each participant of the FizCoin ecosystem has the opportunity to solve problems and send answers to the pool. Each task has its own deadline. After the deadline, the system does not accept responses. It expects a private key within two days to decrypt and verify the responses. After the reconciliation, the calculation of the accrual of remuneration and the accrual itself takes place, if the participant's response is correct.

Before sending a response to the system, the participant freezes the contribution amount in the form of a FIZ token. The participant has the right to change and resend the response before the deadline. After the deadline and receipt by the system of the decryption key of the answers, the participant requests verification and receipt of a reward in the form of a FIZ token.

Participating in idea submission, commentary, proposal submission, and voting is restricted to ApeCoin DAO members. Holding FizCoin is the only requirement for membership in the DAO.

Guiding values

  • Boldness: We don’t shy away from the weird, the hard, or the new.
  • Equality: One FIZ equals one FIZ.
  • Transparency: Processes and decisions are shared openly with the community.
  • Collective Responsibility: We leave everything better than we found it.
  • Persistence: Success is an ouroboros, not a straight line.

COMMUNICATION CHANNELS

The FIZ Foundation website is the DAO hub, providing an interface to educate DAO members on the governance process and provide easy access to the channels below in order to streamline the DAO’s operation and enhance its utility.

Discourse is the first stop for all proposals. An FIP (Fiz Improvement Proposal) Idea is submitted as a post in Discourse and must receive confirmation from a moderator that it complies with DAO-approved guidelines before it appears to the community. FizCoin holders must go through a wallet authentication process to post ideas or give feedback to ideas via comments.

Snapshot is used for voting. Proposal drafts that have passed their respective approval processes become available for voting on Snapshot. ApeCoin holders must go through a wallet authentication process to vote.
